\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Low-\/level wrapper for the physics simulation. 

\doxylink{classgcep_1_1_physics_world}{Physics\+World} manages the internal Jolt \doxylink{classgcep_1_1_physics_system}{Physics\+System} and provides high-\/level functions for body creation, simulation stepping, and physics queries. It also caches collision shapes to reduce redundant allocations.
