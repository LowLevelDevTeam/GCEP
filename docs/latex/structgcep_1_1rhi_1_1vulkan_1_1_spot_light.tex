\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
CPU-\/side description of a spot light. 

A spot light illuminates a cone defined by {\ttfamily inner\+Cutoff\+Deg} and {\ttfamily outer\+Cutoff\+Deg} (both in degrees, measured from the central axis). Between the two angles the intensity is smoothly attenuated using a cosine-\/based falloff. Beyond {\ttfamily radius} the light has no influence.
