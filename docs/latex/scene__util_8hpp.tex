ECS::\+\+Entity\+ID gcep::\+editor::\+spawn\+Asset (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{\doxymbox{\hyperlink{classgcep_1_1_s_l_s_1_1_scene}{SLS::\+\+Scene}} \&}]{scene}{, }\item[{\doxymbox{\hyperlink{classgcep_1_1rhi_1_1vulkan_1_1_vulkan_r_h_i}{rhi::\+vulkan::\+\+Vulkan\+RHI}} \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}}]{rhi}{, }\item[{const std::\+string \&}]{path}{, }\item[{glm::\+vec3}]{pos}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})}



Spawns an external OBJ asset into {\ttfamily scene} at position {\ttfamily pos}. 

Loads the mesh from {\ttfamily path} through the RHI, creates an ECS entity with a {\ttfamily Mesh\+Component} and a {\ttfamily Transform} initialised to {\ttfamily pos}, and registers the entity with the scene manager.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em scene} & Target scene that will own the new entity. \\
\hline
{\em rhi} & Pointer to the active Vulkan RHI used to upload the mesh data. \\
\hline
{\em path} & Filesystem path to the OBJ (or supported mesh) file. \\
\hline
{\em pos} & World-\/space position at which the asset is spawned. \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
The ECS entity ID of the newly created entity. 
\end{DoxyReturn}
