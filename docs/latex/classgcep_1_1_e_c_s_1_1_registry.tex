\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Central Manager for the ECS (Entity Component System). 

The \doxylink{classgcep_1_1_e_c_s_1_1_registry}{Registry} handles the lifecycle of entities, paged allocation of signatures, and maintains the link between entities and their respective components.

Marks an entity for deferred destruction. 

The entity is queued for removal and its ID is invalidated through version checking by the \doxylink{classgcep_1_1_e_c_s_1_1_entity_i_d_generator}{Entity\+IDGenerator}. The entity remains logically valid until the next call to \doxylink{classgcep_1_1_e_c_s_1_1_registry_afeb8f5323d50743039f2374bf4ac9f5f}{update()}, where it is permanently removed and its slot recycled. \begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
The entity remains valid until the next call to \doxylink{classgcep_1_1_e_c_s_1_1_registry_afeb8f5323d50743039f2374bf4ac9f5f}{update()}.
