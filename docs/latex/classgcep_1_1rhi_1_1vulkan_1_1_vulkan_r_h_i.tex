\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Top-\/level Vulkan renderer implementing the {\ttfamily \doxylink{classgcep_1_1_r_h_i}{gcep::\+\+RHI}} interface. 

Private members and helpers are split across {\ttfamily Vulkan\+RHI\+\_\+private\+\_\+\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}}.hpp files included at the bottom of the class to keep this header readable.

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Thread safety}
All public methods must be called from the main/\+render thread. No internal synchronisation is provided. 
\end{DoxyParagraph}
